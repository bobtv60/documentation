Upview provides a powerful, schema-driven feedback system that bridges the gap between game design and player sentiment. Whether you want a no-code solution or full control over your UI, Upview has you covered.

## The Three Tiers of Integration

Choose the level of control that fits your team's workflow.

### Why use Upview Feedback?

- **Automatic Context**: Every submission captures FPS, Memory usage, Server Version, and Player identity automatically.
- **Dynamic Schemas**: Update your questions in the cloud without patching your game.
- **Aesthetic Flexibility**: Use our built-in premium themes or bind to your own custom UI.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: All text responses are automatically analyzed for sentiment score.
